FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

What services does Luxenest Properties offer?+

We offer short-term rental management, co-hosting, interior styling, cleaning coordination, listing optimisation, and owner/investor support across South Africa.

How do you manage properties remotely?+

We handle everything remotely and on-site — guest communications, check-ins, cleaning coordination and platform management. You stay hands-off while we maximise returns.

What's the difference between the 25% and 30% plan?+

The 25% Standard plan covers full listing management and guest communications. The 30% Full Service plan adds cleaning coordination, linen management, interior guidance and monthly owner reports.

Can you help with interior design for my property?+

Yes. We provide interior styling guidance focused on luxury presentation, guest appeal and photography readiness to help your listing stand out and command premium rates.

How do I get started with Luxenest Properties?+

Simply fill out our contact form and we'll schedule a property review call to assess your property and recommend the right management plan for your goals.
