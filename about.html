Meet the Founder of

Luxenest Properties is a refined real estate brand founded by Anesu Chekururama, delivering premium property solutions with precision and discretion — connecting clients to spaces that reflect their lifestyle, vision, and long-term value.

Through Luxenest Properties, she continues to build a brand that reflects modern luxury, operational excellence, and trusted expertise in the property space — curated selection, seamless processes, and confidential client-focused service.

FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

What services does Luxenest Properties offer?+

We offer short-term rental management, co-hosting, interior styling, cleaning coordination, listing optimisation, and owner/investor support across South Africa.

How do you manage properties in Johannesburg?+

We handle everything remotely and on-site — guest communications, check-ins, cleaning coordination and platform management. You stay hands-off while we maximise returns.

What's included in your co-hosting service?+

Co-hosting includes platform listing management, guest communication, review management, and booking coordination on Airbnb, Booking.com and other OTAs.

Can you help with interior design for my property?+

Yes. We provide interior styling guidance focused on luxury presentation, guest appeal and photography readiness to help your listing stand out.

How do I get started with Luxenest Properties?+

Simply fill out our contact form and we'll schedule a property review call. We'll assess your property and recommend the right management plan for you.
